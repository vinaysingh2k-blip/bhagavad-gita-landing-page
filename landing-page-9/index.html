Duties arise from the qualities already born in each person's own nature. Purpose is discovered by paying honest attention to your actual disposition, including its inconvenient parts.

Chapter 07 · 18.45

Fulfillment comes from ordinary, repeated devotion to your own work — not a single dramatic leap. Purpose accumulates as a practice, not an event.
