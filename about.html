About Us

Ancient Wisdom, Built for Modern Life

Krishna Vani exists to make the Bhagavad Gita's teachings genuinely usable — not just read once and shelved, but returned to during the actual decisions, pressures, and quiet moments of everyday life.

Why We Started

Most people who pick up the Bhagavad Gita for the first time face the same two problems: the language feels dense and unfamiliar, and it's not always obvious how a 5,000-year-old dialogue on a battlefield connects to a hard conversation with a manager, a restless mind at 2am, or a decision about money. We built Krishna Vani to close that gap — presenting the Gita's actual text faithfully where that matters, and offering focused, honest companions inspired by its themes where a reader needs something more immediately applicable.

What We Publish

Our catalog covers two distinct kinds of editions, and we're upfront about the difference on every product page:

  • Complete illustrated editions — the Bhagavad Gita's actual text, with Sanskrit shlokas, Hindi translation, and English meaning for every chapter.
  • The Practical Guide Series — original commentary inspired by the Gita's themes, each chapter citing a specific verse alongside a practical exercise, built around one focus area at a time: career, discipline, health, relationships, and more.

Who We Are

Krishna Vani is a brand operated by Growthyfai Technologies Private Limited. We're a small team focused on one thing: making timeless wisdom easy to actually use.
