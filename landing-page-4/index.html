The Four Paths to the Divine

The Gita weaves together four great paths — not as rivals, but as complementary currents of a single river. Each temperament may enter by the path most natural to it.

⚙️

Karma Yoga

The Path of Action

Duty performed without craving for its fruit, offered to the Divine, purifies the mind and severs the root of bondage.

❤️

Bhakti Yoga

The Path of Devotion

The heart, poured out to the Beloved in every act and every breath, is lifted beyond fear and sorrow.

🧠

Jnana Yoga

The Path of Wisdom

Discrimination between the eternal Self and the changing world dissolves ignorance, the sole cause of suffering.

🧘

Dhyana Yoga

The Path of Meditation

Steady inward gathering of the mind reveals the Self as ever-present, ever-luminous, ever-free.
