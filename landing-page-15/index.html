The Solution

The Complete Text, Made Easy to Follow.

This edition presents the Hanuman Chalisa exactly as it has been recited for close to five hundred years — every chaupai in its original Awadhi/Sanskrit form, a Roman transliteration, a clear Hindi meaning, and an English translation — organized into eight short sections so the text is easier to study, reflect on, and return to.
